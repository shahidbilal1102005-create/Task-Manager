Privacy Policy

Task Manager - Your Privacy Matters

Information We Collect

Task Manager respects your privacy. We collect and store only the information you explicitly provide:

  • Task Data: Titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities, and completion status
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or location data

How We Use Your Data

All your task data is stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your data to any server
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for analytics or marketing
  • Access your personal files or contacts

✅ 100% Local Storage: Your tasks never leave your device.

Data Storage

Task Manager uses Hive database for local storage:

  • All data is stored exclusively on your device
  • No cloud synchronization or backup
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can manually delete individual tasks at any time

Permissions

Task Manager requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: Only for saving app data locally
  • Internet: Not required for core functionality

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • No internet connection required for app functionality
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • Your tasks remain private on your device only
  • We recommend using device lock for additional security

Children's Privacy

Task Manager is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data is device-local and parent-controlled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
